Welcome to Serenity

Craving delicious Chinese, American, and Japanese cuisine? You can have these delectable foods in just one restaurant! Hibachi Grill & Supreme Buffet of Jackson, TN is your favorite buffet restaurant that offers a wide range of meals for lunch, dinner, take-out, and even catering. We offer a variety of foods from asian culture and much more! Make sure to check out the menu to see our broad selection!

On this website, you can order by phone anything on our menu and we will make sure to have it ready for you when you pick it up. You can also have a seat and enjoy the all-you-can-eat buffet and sushi bar. Come on by!
