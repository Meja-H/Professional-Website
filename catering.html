Throw a Party!

At Hibachi Grill and Supreme Buffet, we are glad to cater to any sort of event. We have previously catered to bat mitzvahs, family events, and even birthdays. However, there are some things you need to do before-hand before ordering. Here are our regulations for catering:

  • Please:
  • -Order catering service 2 weeks prior to event.
  • -Make a deposit before the event.
  • -Be specific with what food to be catered to event.
  • -Be 18 years of age or older to order catering services.
