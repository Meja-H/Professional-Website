    Here at Hibachi Grill & Supreme Buffet, we have a selection of over 200 food items a day which includes the grill, sushi bar, and desert. Everything for a price of $11.99 for Lunch and $15.99 for Dinner.

    We serve a variety of asian cuisine to appease anyone's taste. We have a beef, chicken, fish, pork, and for the vegetarians, tofu. Not to mention our delicious spring rolls (picture above).

    Our chefs have been trained in the art of taste, so all of our food is not only good to look at but is also delicious. Dont be afraid to try something new like our fried squid. You will definitely find tasty to eat.

    Here at Hibachi Grill & Supreme Buffet, we take pride in having an orderly and clean buffet. We have waiters working to make sure all the food is in your convenience, so please take all you can eat!
